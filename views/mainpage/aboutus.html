Our Story

In 2004, MIT graduate students Brian Halligan and Dharmesh Shah noticed a major shift in the way people shop and purchase products. Buyers didn't want to be interrupted by ads; they wanted helpful information.

In 2006, they founded HubSpot to help companies use that shift to grow better with inbound marketing.
